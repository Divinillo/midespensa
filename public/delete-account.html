Delete Your Account

We're sorry to see you go. You can delete your MiDespensa account and all associated data at any time by following the steps below.

How to delete your account

  1. Open MiDespensa at midespensa.app
  2. Sign in to your account
  3. Go to Settings (gear icon in the top right)
  4. Scroll down and tap "Delete Account"
  5. Confirm the deletion when prompted

Data that will be permanently deleted

  • Your email address and profile information
  • Pantry items and inventory
  • Meal plans and saved recipes
  • Shopping lists
  • Subscription and preference settings

Data retention

  • All personal data is deleted immediately upon account deletion
  • Anonymized, aggregated analytics data may be retained
  • No personal data is shared with third parties

Delete specific data without removing your account

You can also delete specific data without deleting your entire account. In the app, go to Settings and use the options to clear your pantry, meal plans, or shopping lists individually.

Need help?

If you have trouble deleting your account or want to request data deletion by email, contact us at contacto@midespensa.app. We'll process your request within 48 hours.
